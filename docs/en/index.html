A Finnish counterpart to Anthropic’s “Claude for Legal”.

Open-source skills, agents and guardrails that make Claude a sharper, better assistant for Finnish legal professionals. Every feature is built on real-time source-checking, three-tier certainty labelling and a human-in-the-loop review gate.

01

Protection comes from mechanisms

From the source, not from memory

Statutes and case law are fetched from Finlex and a legal-source MCP (oik.ai or laki.ai). Every statute number in the repo is verified against the source before release.

Three-tier certainty

Verified · To be checked · Do not use — every citation and calculation carries its certainty label on its own line.

Negative scoping

Every skill states what it does NOT do: no cartel help, no destroying evidence, no invented statutes, no covering up crime.

Human review gate

Every output is a draft. A qualified human checks, decides and bears responsibility — the tool speeds up review, it does not replace it.

03

Agents — delegable work steps

Source checkerjuristi · lahdetarkastaja

Assumes every citation is wrong until the source proves otherwise. Returns a verification table with fix suggestions — run it on every document that goes out.

Deadline scannerjuristi · maaraaikaskanneri

Scans every deadline in the material, calculates them on the cautious principle from both possible start dates, and surfaces the most urgent first. Calendar responsibility stays with the human.

Opposing counselriidanratkaisu · vastapuoli

A simulator of the other side’s counsel: attacks your brief in earnest and returns a weakness table, your strengths and the top 3 fixes before filing.

Data-room mapperyhtiöoikeus · aineistokartoittaja

Inventories the data room by DD area, produces a gap list ready as an information request and red flags for prioritisation. An inventory — not a legal assessment.

Deviation checkersopimukset · poikkeamatarkastaja

Compares a draft against house risk policy clause by clause and points out what to escalate. Stops if there is no yardstick — it does not invent “market practice”.

Secrecy assessorhallinto-oikeus · salassapitoarvioija

Prepares a transparency assessment point by point with statutory grounds and produces a redaction proposal. Partial disclosure as the rule; the official decides.

Install in the desktop app (Cowork)

  1. Open the desktop app
  2. Go to the Cowork tab
  3. Open Customize
  4. Choose Add plugin
  5. Choose Add marketplace and paste akunikkola/claude-for-legal-finland

For organisations

Before production use: a data policy, a processing agreement (GDPR Art. 28), anonymisation as a workflow (PII Shield) and a named review chain — references/kayttoonotto-toimistossa.md. House conventions are recorded with the kaytantoprofiili skill; pilot with the example datasets, which include assessment criteria.

Get involved

Contribute Sponsor the project

Every output is a draft for review. This is not legal advice, and neither this collection nor its features replace a lawyer. The user always bears responsibility for the outcome, and use is recommended only for trained legal professionals. The plugins are built accordingly: law and case law are checked against the source rather than from memory, every citation carries a source mark, jurisdiction assumptions are kept visible, and before anything is sent or signed a human verifies it. Protection comes from mechanisms — source verification, three-tier certainty labelling, premise checking, negative scoping and the human review gate. The mechanisms are collected in references/viittaustyyli.md and references/vastuu-ja-tietoturva.md.

This is an open community project — not an authority, legal or attorney service — and it does not represent any organisation’s official legal position. Anyone can contribute their own skill files and plugins that have proven useful in their own work.
